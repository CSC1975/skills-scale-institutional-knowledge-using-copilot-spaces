# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Quality Assurance Lead

### Role Summary
Quality Assurance Leads define and oversee quality standards, testing strategies, and acceptance validation. They ensure that deliverables meet acceptance criteria and quality requirements before release.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and quality gates for projects
- Create and maintain test plans and test cases
- Validate features against acceptance criteria
- Identify quality risks and recommend mitigations
- Lead test automation efforts and maintain test infrastructure
- Coordinate with Developers on defect resolution

### Goals
- Ensure high quality and reliability of deliverables
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Maintain comprehensive test coverage
- Reduce production incidents through rigorous validation

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and quality status reports
- Defect reports and triage discussions
- Sign-off on acceptance criteria and release readiness
- Test automation metrics and coverage reports

### Interaction with other roles
- **Developers**: Collaborate on testability, review test cases, coordinate on defect fixes
- **Product Managers**: Validate acceptance criteria, provide quality insights on feature readiness
- **Project Managers**: Report quality metrics, flag quality risks, coordinate test schedules
- **Stakeholders**: Provide quality status updates and release readiness assessments

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user-centered designs that balance usability, accessibility, and business goals. They translate user needs into intuitive interfaces and ensure consistent design patterns across products.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Define design systems and style guides
- Ensure accessibility standards are met
- Collaborate with Product Managers and Developers on feasibility
- Validate implemented designs match specifications

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Ensure product-market fit through user research
- Maintain design consistency across features
- Reduce friction in user workflows

### Typical Communication
- Design specs and prototypes
- User research findings and personas
- Usability test results and recommendations
- Design review sessions with stakeholders

### Interaction with other roles
- **Developers**: Provide design specifications, review implementations, clarify design intent
- **Product Managers**: Collaborate on user needs, validate solutions through research, balance design and business goals
- **Project Managers**: Coordinate design timelines, flag design dependencies
- **Stakeholders**: Present design concepts, gather feedback, align on user experience vision

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain infrastructure, deployment pipelines, and operational tooling. They enable rapid, reliable delivery through automation and ensure system reliability and observability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure as code and cloud resources
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Automate deployment and operational tasks
- Ensure security and compliance in infrastructure
- Support incident response and troubleshooting

### Goals
- Reduce deployment time and increase release frequency
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Enable self-service infrastructure for development teams
- Maintain security and compliance standards

### Typical Communication
- Deployment runbooks and operational documentation
- Infrastructure change requests and reviews
- Incident reports and postmortems
- System performance and reliability metrics

### Interaction with other roles
- **Developers**: Provide deployment tooling, support troubleshooting, collaborate on system architecture
- **Product Managers**: Communicate infrastructure constraints, provide deployment timelines
- **Project Managers**: Report on infrastructure readiness, coordinate deployment schedules, manage infrastructure risks
- **Stakeholders**: Provide system reliability reports and infrastructure capacity planning
